What I did on Sunday

Left not so early in the morning to go to an amazing walk through a small pueblo named Breda for breakfast. Followed by an awesome tour of an old castle . This tour was done in catalan so I even got a chance to practice my foreign language more. After seeing many cool features and hearing many stories (that I need much clarification for) we ran down the hill to get to the car to make it to the restaurant in time for our reservation. After an extremely tasty lunch we drove back to Barcelona just in time to siesta and wake up to watch athletico madrid kick real madrids ass in el derbi.
